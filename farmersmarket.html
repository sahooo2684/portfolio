Farmers Market: Direct-to-Consumer Produce Platform

Description: Farmers Market is a digital platform that bridges the gap between local farmers and urban consumers by enabling direct sales of fresh produce without intermediaries. The system helps promote fair pricing, transparent transactions, and community-supported agriculture.

Key Features:

  • Farmer and consumer registration with profile verification
  • Product listing, availability, and pricing tools for farmers
  • Search and filter produce by type, freshness, and location
  • Secure checkout and delivery request system
  • Basic reviews and rating functionality

Technologies Used: HTML, CSS, PHP, MySQL, Bootstrap
